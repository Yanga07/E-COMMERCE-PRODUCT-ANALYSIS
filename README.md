### Key Insights

üèÜ Top 5 Performing Brands

- TechCorp, StyleHouse, and TrendSetter lead revenue generation.

- These brands show consistent sales above $4M.

üíº Category Performance

- Luxury Goods is the most profitable category, followed by Cosmetics and Electronics.

- Revenue differences between top and mid-tier categories highlight potential for targeted promotions.

üïí Shipping & Waiting Time

- Most orders (‚âà 38%) are delivered within 2 weeks, while 1-week and 3-week deliveries make up smaller shares.

- This distribution indicates strong logistics efficiency for mid-term shipping.

üöö Dominant Shipping Origins

- Top origins: KR (Korea), CA (Canada), CN (China), US, and DE (Germany).

- Korea and Canada contribute the most shipped products, indicating major supplier bases.

‚è≥ Revenue by Product Lifetime

- Products generate the highest revenue in their first year of existence.

- Revenue gradually declines after 5 years, suggesting lifecycle saturation.

üßæ Warranty Duration

- Majority of products fall under short-term and long-term warranties.

- Balanced distribution across all warranty categories indicates diverse product strategies.


### Filters and Interactivity

Users can dynamically filter the data based on:

- Fake products

- Bulk orders

- Return allowance

- Unclear payments

- Full contact availability

These filters enhance targeted analysis for fraud detection, operational issues, or customer behavior studies.
